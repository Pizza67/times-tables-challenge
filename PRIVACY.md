# Privacy policy

This app does not collect or use any personal data from users. 

This means that *we do not collect information such as name, email address, location, browsing history or any other type of information that could personally identify a user*.

The app is completely ads-free and is designed to work completely offline and does not send any information to external servers. 

We do not use cookies, trackers or other tracking technologies.

Your privacy is important to us and we are committed to protecting it. 

If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy policy, please do not hesitate to contact us.
